Inspect Site

I first started working on this site on May 26, 2022 after lurking and reaping inspiration from Olia Lialina and Dragan Espenschied's archive of Geocities, as well as Kyle Drake's revival of the framework on Neocities.

In accordance to the old-school structures of the aforementioned sites, this website is (for now) composed entirely of HTML and CSS. Its minimal design is intentional. My first experience learning HTML was in middle school when I began to tinker with themes on Tumblr. I've learned much since then and will continue to learn more. With that said, I want this to be a forever WIP project that grows and changes with me as I expand my skill set. In other words, I'm a fucking webmaster.
